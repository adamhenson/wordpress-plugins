Display images from your Instagram account with ease in a simple, elegant, responsive layout.

== Description ==

Within the Wordpress admin choose images from your Instagram account to show. You can also display a feed of your most recent posts to show in real time. The presentation includes upd to date like count, comment count, caption, and profile pic.
